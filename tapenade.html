Tapenade

Tapenade is a Provençal name for a spread, condiment and culinary ingredient consisting of puréed or finely chopped olives, capers, and anchovies. Its name comes from the Provençal word for capers, tapenas

Spread on crackers, pita chips, crackers, or bread. Stuff portobello mushrooms with the tapenade. Mix it in with some sauce with pasta, polenta, or gnocchi. Enjoy with some fresh veggies- carrots, celery, etc

It will blow your mind!
