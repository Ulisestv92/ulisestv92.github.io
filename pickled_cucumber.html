Pickled cucumber

A pickled cucumber is a cucumber that has been pickled in a brine, vinegar, or other solution and left to ferment for a period of time, by either immersing the cucumbers in an acidic solution or through souring by lacto-fermentation.

Our jar contains 500 gr of cucumber pickles. It has a unique flavor, as we use Middle Eastern spices that give it a distinctive aroma. We use turmeric, cloves, coriando, mustard grains, pepper, and a mixture of selected vinegars.
