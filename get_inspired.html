Mise en place

Sometimes it is not easy to take the first step in the kitchen, that is why we are here to help you. Firsty, you should think about what you want to try and with whom you would like to share it.

Secondly, you must analyze getting all the ingredients and raw materials to be able to make the recipe. It is very important to do the 'mise en place' before starting to cook. This process consists of assembling everything prior to cooking, from cutting all the vegetables, separating the spices, cleaning any cut of meat, etc.

Now you can start with the step by step recipe of the dish that we are going to prepare!
