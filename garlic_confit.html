Garlic confit

A jar of garlic confit is a great staple to have in your fridge. Perfect for blending through dips, dressings or a simple pasta sauce, our favourites include extra-garlicky hummus and an unctuous garlic pesto.

Our jar contains 250g of candied garlic. We add various seasonings to create a unique flavor and aroma. You can use it to cook different dishes or simply enjoy it with a good slice of bread.

Simply great!
